SCUT-FBP - Classification

Classification was performed by rounding att_level values to obtain integers. The SCUT-FBP dataset scores range from 0 to 5. The results for accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score are shown below for both male and female subsets.

SCUT-FBP

Females

Accuracy

The accuracy of different models is compared. The Decision Tree and Random Forest models (both normalized and non-normalized) show the highest accuracy. The K-Nearest Neighbors model, particularly the non-normalized version, has the lowest accuracy.

Precision

Precision results indicate that the Decision Tree and Random Forest models (both normalized and non-normalized) perform better in identifying relevant instances among the retrieved ones. The Support Vector Classifier and K-Nearest Neighbors models lag behind in precision.

Recall

Recall results show that the Decision Tree and Random Forest models have the highest recall, meaning they are better at capturing all relevant instances. The K-Nearest Neighbors model has the lowest recall among the models.

F1 Score

The F1 score, which is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, is highest for the Decision Tree and Random Forest models. The K-Nearest Neighbors model shows the lowest F1 score, indicating a balance between low precision and recall.

Males

Accuracy

The Decision Tree and Random Forest models again show the highest accuracy for the male subset, similar to the female subset. K-Nearest Neighbors model has relatively lower accuracy.

Precision

Precision results for males are consistent with those for females, with Decision Tree and Random Forest models leading in performance. The K-Nearest Neighbors model has the lowest precision among the evaluated models.

Recall

Recall results for males indicate that the Decision Tree and Random Forest models have the highest recall, capturing most of the relevant instances. The K-Nearest Neighbors model shows the lowest recall.

F1 Score

The F1 score for males mirrors the trend seen in females, with Decision Tree and Random Forest models achieving the highest scores. The K-Nearest Neighbors model has the lowest F1 score.

MeBeauty

Females

F1 Score
